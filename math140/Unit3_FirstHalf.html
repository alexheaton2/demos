Composite, Implicit, and Inverse Functions

Key Derivative Rules to Know

Exponential & Log

  • d/dx (a^x) = a^x * ln(a)
  • d/dx (log_a(x)) = 1 / (x * ln(a))

Sine & Cosine

  • d/dx (sin(x)) = cos(x)
  • d/dx (cos(x)) = -sin(x)

Tangent & Cotangent

  • d/dx (tan(x)) = sec^2(x)
  • d/dx (cot(x)) = -csc^2(x)

Secant & Cosecant

  • d/dx (sec(x)) = sec(x)tan(x)
  • d/dx (csc(x)) = -csc(x)cot(x)

Note: You will be given these derivative rules as part of a "cheat sheet" to use during exams. The focus is on applying them, especially with the chain rule!
